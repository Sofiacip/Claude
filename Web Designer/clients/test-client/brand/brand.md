# Brand Document — Pattie Ehsaei

## Colors
Primary (Duchess Burgundy): #8B1A3A   <!-- CTAs, headline accents, dominant power tone -->
Old Gold:                   #C9963A   <!-- Borders, dividers, decorative rules, overlines -->
Champagne:                  #F5E6D0   <!-- Hero and section backgrounds -->
Blush Peach:                #E8C4B0   <!-- Secondary backgrounds, form panel warmth -->
Background (Ivory):         #FAF5EE   <!-- Primary page background — never pure white -->
Text (Charcoal):            #2C2C2C   <!-- Body text — never pure black -->
Rich Black:                 #000000   <!-- High-contrast UI elements, logos -->
White:                      #FFFFFF   <!-- Text on dark/burgundy backgrounds -->
Mid Gray:                   #888888   <!-- Captions, fine print, subtext -->

## Typography
Heading Font: Cormorant Garamond
  Used for: Hero titles, H2, H3 section openers, pull quotes
  Weights: Regular, SemiBold (600), Bold (700)
  Style: Sentence case (never ALL CAPS for display). High-contrast serif, literary, editorial.

Body Font: Montserrat
  Used for: Body copy, labels, nav, form fields, CTA buttons, captions
  Weights: 400, 500, 600, 700, 800
  Labels/overlines: ALL CAPS, letter-spacing 0.12em
  Body paragraphs: Mixed case

## Type Scale
Hero Headline:     Cormorant Garamond, 56–72px, Bold — sentence case, white or burgundy on dark
Section H2:        Cormorant Garamond, 36–48px, SemiBold — dark charcoal or burgundy
Overline/Label:    Montserrat, 11–13px, 700, ALL CAPS, letter-spacing 0.12em, gold or gray
Subheading H3:     Montserrat, 16–20px, 700, ALL CAPS
Body Copy:         Montserrat, 15–17px, 400 — charcoal on light, line-height ~1.7
CTA Button:        Montserrat, 14–16px, 700, ALL CAPS, letter-spacing 0.1em
Caption:           Montserrat, 11–12px, 400, mid-gray

## Buttons
Primary CTA: #8B1A3A background, white ALL CAPS Montserrat text, letter-spacing 0.1em, border-radius 2–4px
Secondary: White or transparent bg, burgundy border, burgundy text

## Forms
Fields: White or off-white bg, thin gold or light gray border, gray placeholder text
Labels: Small Montserrat ALL CAPS above input
Submit: Full-width burgundy CTA style

## Logo
Wordmark: "Pattie Ehsaei" in Cormorant Garamond (no SVG file available — render as text wordmark)

## Decorative Elements
- Diamond bullet ◆ as section separator / accent character in headers
- Numbered lists use large Cormorant Garamond display numbers (01, 02, 03...) as visual anchors
- Rotating badge/stamp SVGs near form sections (render as CSS animated circular badge)

## Brand Personality
High-end editorial. Harper's Bazaar meets Harvard Business Review.
Authority + femininity + warmth. Luxury personal brand, not corporate.
Max-width: 900–1100px. Generous section padding: 80–120px top/bottom.
Full-bleed alternating sections: ivory → champagne → dark → ivory

## Key Brand Terms
- AccompliSHE (capitalize SHE — her program name)
- Never Date a Broke Dude (italicized — her book title)
- @duchessofdecorum (her social handle — bold Montserrat)
- "No Romance Without Finance" (her philosophy/tagline)
- Deliberately broken headline words (MNEY, LVE, etc.) are intentional — do NOT correct them

## Don'ts
- No cool blues, greens, purples, or high-saturation colors
- No pure white (#FFF) for backgrounds — use Ivory (#FAF5EE)
- No pure black (#000) for body text — use Charcoal (#2C2C2C)
- No geometric/playful fonts — Cormorant Garamond + Montserrat only
- Design must feel powerful AND feminine
