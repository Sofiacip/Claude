What You'll Learn

This is not a general overview. This is a specific, structured breakdown of the five things that have been quietly shaping your financial reality, and exactly what to do about them.

01

The 5 core lies women were told about money, men, and the patriarchal system

Most women have absorbed a set of financial beliefs that were never designed to help them succeed. Pattie names them clearly and explains how each one shows up in your day-to-day decisions.

02

Why your financial insecurity is a learned system, not a personal failure

If you have been blaming yourself for not being further ahead, this will change everything. Financial dependence is not a character flaw. It is the predictable result of a system working exactly as it was designed to.

03

How internalized patriarchy quietly shapes your career, your relationships, and your bank balance

This is the piece most people miss. Internalized patriarchy does not just live in culture. It lives in how you negotiate, who you defer to in financial conversations, and what you unconsciously believe you deserve.

04

The shift from gatherer to hunter, and what it means for how you earn

Pattie's hunter vs. gatherer framework is one of the most practical reframes women take out of this session. It changes how you show up in salary negotiations, in business, and in relationships with money.

05

Your first practical steps toward real financial control, starting this week

You will not leave with a vague sense of inspiration. You will leave with a clear, immediate action plan for taking the first steps toward financial independence on your own terms.
