The Framework

Discover the 3 Phases To Navigate
the Hurt of Betrayal and Move Forward Together

This workshop uses the proven 3-phase Relational Life Therapy framework — guiding both partners through healing, understanding, and genuine transformation.

Phase 01

Recover From Shattered Trust

Addresses the hurt partner's burning questions — How could you? How do I know you won't again? We create space to grieve the old relationship and begin piecing reality back together.

Phase 02

Uncover the Meaning Behind Infidelity

We shift from "what happened" to "why it happened." What overrode this person's capacity to say no? We move from shame and blame into genuine accountability and empathy.

Phase 03

Use the Crisis for Transformation

Identify the relationship issues that need work. Learn critical communication skills — how to express hurt, listen non-defensively, and build the trust-restoring behaviors that stick.
